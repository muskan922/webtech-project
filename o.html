Frequently Asked Questions

How do I update my profile? Click on "Profile Settings" in the sidebar to edit your personal information, address, and other details.
Is my data secure? This is a demo application that stores data in your browser's localStorage. In a production app, data would be encrypted and stored securely on servers.
How do I change my password? Go to "Security" in the sidebar, enter your current password, then set and confirm your new password.
